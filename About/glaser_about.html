Stephanie Russell - About

The Story So Far...

Stephanie Russell is a 25-year-old student at Bellevue University who is currently working on a bachelor’s in graphics design. She hails from a small town in Iowa called Waverly where life was slower than she would have liked. She spent most of her childhood hanging out with friends and family and playing outside. She is most like her mother in that she is quiet and reserved until you get to know her. She often worries about things that are out of her control but is energized to confront any problems in front of her. In high school, she was a part of band as a clarinet player and also was a soprano in the school Choir. In her free time, she enjoys developing her artistic skills and playing with her two cats, Icarus and Gidget. Her first job was working as a dietary aid at a nursing home. She assisted with residents’ dietary needs and enjoyed conversing with the residents. Her dream is to become a full-time artist once she graduates from college. She hopes to move to Seattle someday once she can settle down. She fell in love with the city’s climate and lush atmosphere. Her biggest role model is her close friend Emily, who lives life to the fullest every day. The once advice she would give her younger self is “You have more talent than you think”.
